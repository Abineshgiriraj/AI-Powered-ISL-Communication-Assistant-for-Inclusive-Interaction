About the Project

Empowering inclusive communication through advanced AI technology

This assistant leverages modern AI models to enable seamless communication between spoken language, Indian Sign Language (ISL), and text. By combining recognition, translation, and synthesis in a privacy-conscious pipeline, it supports inclusive interactions across diverse contexts, making communication accessible for everyone in our community.

Key Features

Built for reliability, accessibility, and real-world impact

🎤

Speech to ISL

Convert spoken input into clear, intelligible ISL output for accessible conversations with real-time processing and high accuracy.

✋

ISL to Text/Speech

Recognize ISL gestures and translate them into readable text and natural-sounding speech with advanced computer vision.

⚡

AI-Powered Models

Utilizes modern AI for robust recognition, translation, and synthesis in varied environments with continuous learning capabilities.

☁

Cloud Integration

Flexible deployment with cloud services for scalability, latency reduction, and reliability across multiple platforms.

🎨

Inclusive Design

Prioritizes clarity, contrast, and assistive-friendly interactions for all users with accessibility-first approach.

How It Works

A simple three-step pipeline from input to output

1

Input

Capture speech, video (ISL), or text as the starting point for translation using advanced sensors and microphones.

2

Processing

AI models analyze and translate content through recognition, mapping, and synthesis using neural networks and machine learning.

3

Output

Deliver ISL animations, synthesized speech, or readable text—based on user needs with high fidelity and natural flow.
